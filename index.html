Short summary

Koperasi Delapan Lima was established on 20 May 2013. Initial member was 137.

Members of Koperasi Delapan Lima (KDL) are alumnus of Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) class of 1985. Currently we have 250 member and managing assets worth around 2.3 billion rupiahs.

KDL is a forum for exchanging information and strengthening relationship among its member as well as broader alumnus of ITB. We supported the activities of members, provide assistance and also established business venture to channel the entrepreneurship of our members.

Annual Member Meeting

Annual gathering for member to review achievement of current elected Board and vote for new Board if necessary. The last annual meeting was conducted online in April 2021.

KDL Sharing

We invited speakers either from the member or from successful person to share their experiences so our members can learn and applied the knowledge.

Cafe Sunyi

Business venture of KDL in collaboration with Sunyi House of Coffee and Hope. We established a coffee shop at Bekasi and employed disabled baristas.
